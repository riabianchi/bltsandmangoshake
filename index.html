Mango Shake

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe mango, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (optional)

Procedure:

  1. Place the diced mango, milk, ice cubes, and sugar (if using) in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.

BLT Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of bacon
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Cook the bacon until crispy, then drain on paper towels.
  2. To assemble the sandwich, spread mayonnaise on one slice of bread.
  3. Layer lettuce leaves, sliced tomato, and cooked bacon on top of the mayonnaise.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, then top with the second slice of bread.
  5. Cut the sandwich in half and serve.
